Everything new families want to know about joining Elite Dance & Music

We welcome dancers from age 2 through 18! Our Tiny Dancers program starts at age 2, and we have classes for preschoolers, elementary, middle school, high school, and adults. Each age group has specially designed curriculum appropriate for their developmental stage.
