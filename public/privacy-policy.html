Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-09-01

Elite Dance LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while visiting our website (myelitedance.com).

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and details you submit through forms.
  • Payment Information: Handled securely via Stripe, PayPal, or our registration platform.
  • Usage Data: Browser info, IP address, and interactions collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Social Media Info: If you log in or connect via platforms like Facebook or Instagram.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and manage class registrations and customer support
  • Send updates, confirmations, and promotional emails
  • Improve the user experience and our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., email, hosting, payment processors)
  • Law enforcement or regulators when legally required
  • Social platforms if you voluntarily connect through them

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to enhance your experience. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement safeguards to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure. Please take care when sharing data online.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us to remove it.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access, updates, or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

9. California Privacy Rights

California residents may request access to personal data, ask for deletion, or request more details under the CCPA. We do not sell personal data.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
